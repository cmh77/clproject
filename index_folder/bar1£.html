Why eating here?

Our rockin’ bar and diner sets an awesome contrast to Bath’s historical backdrop. Quite literally on the doorstep of the city’s most famed attractions, our Bath Belushi’s boasts a badass food and drink menu offering killer breakfasts, a truckload of burgers and a mammoth choice on local ales, ciders, spirits and international craft beers.


What makes the menu so special is that they have a so-called 'rebel' section. Meals in this section have really wierd names and wierd combinations of ingredients and saurces. Why not give a try? Never too old to take a risk on new things!
